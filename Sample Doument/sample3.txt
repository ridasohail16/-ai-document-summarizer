Climate change is one of the most serious environmental challenges facing our planet today. Rising global temperatures, melting ice caps, and extreme weather events are direct consequences of increasing greenhouse gas emissions caused by human activities. Scientists warn that if emissions are not reduced significantly, global temperatures could rise to catastrophic levels within the next few decades. Coastal cities around the world face the threat of flooding as sea levels continue to rise, while other regions experience severe droughts and food shortages. Governments and organizations worldwide are working together to combat climate change through renewable energy, sustainable agriculture, and stricter environmental policies. Every individual can also contribute by reducing energy consumption and adopting eco-friendly habits in daily life.
